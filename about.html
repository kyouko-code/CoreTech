ABOUT CORE TEACH

Bringing technology education and quality computers to Battambang, Cambodia since 2020

OUR STORY

CoreTeach was founded in Battambang with a simple mission: to make quality computing technology accessible and understandable to everyone in our community.

What started as a small computer repair shop has grown into a full-service technology provider, offering custom-built computers, laptops, parts, and educational resources.

We believe that understanding technology shouldn't be complicated. That's why we focus on teaching our customers about their devices, not just selling them.

OUR VALUES

EDUCATION FIRST

We teach you how to use and maintain your technology, empowering you with knowledge.

QUALITY BUILDS

Every system we build uses quality components and undergoes rigorous testing.

LOCAL SUPPORT

We're based right here in Battambang, offering in-person support and service.

SUSTAINABILITY

We promote upgradable systems and repair over replacement to reduce e-waste.
