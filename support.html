FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I claim my warranty?

You can claim warranty by visiting our store in Battambang with your purchase receipt, or by contacting our support team via email. All CoreTeach products come with a 3-year limited warranty.

Do you offer repair services?

Yes! We have a repair center in Battambang where our technicians can service any CoreTeach product. We also offer repair services for other brands at competitive rates.

Can I upgrade my CoreTeach computer?

Absolutely! Our computers are designed to be upgradeable. You can purchase compatible parts from our website or visit our store for assistance with upgrades.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes, students with valid ID can get 10% off on laptops and pre-built systems. Visit our store in Battambang with your student ID to claim the discount.
